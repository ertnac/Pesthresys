Frequently Asked Questions How do I add a new plant?
To add a new plant, go to your Plant Collection and tap the '+' button. You can either take a photo of your plant or select from our database.
How does pest detection work?
Our AI analyzes photos of your plants to identify common pests and diseases. For best results, take clear photos of affected leaves from multiple angles.
How often should I water my plants?
Watering needs vary by plant type, season, and environment. The app provides personalized recommendations based on your specific plants and conditions.
