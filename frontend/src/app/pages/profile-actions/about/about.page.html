About Us

Pesthresys is an intelligent plant care assistant that helps you maintain healthy plants by detecting pests and diseases early.

Our mission is to make plant care accessible to everyone, from novice gardeners to expert horticulturists.

 Technology

Pesthresys uses advanced machine learning algorithms to analyze plant health and identify issues before they become serious problems.

Our database contains information on thousands of plant species, pests, and diseases to provide accurate recommendations.
